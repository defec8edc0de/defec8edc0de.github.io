Hi my name is Sebastian, I'm security researcher, bitcoin enthusiast, defender of informational privacy and computer science student in the master program IT Security at TU Darmstadt in Germany.

I have been working on different kind of projects like: several web applications, automated RF-Testing, a privacy analysis of Facebook (BSc Thesis) and several other software projects with a variety of technologies.

My current passion is privacy in Internet of Things and all kind of IT security related topics, especially web security, secure software development and wireless physical layer security.

Professional, I research and build Privacy Enhancing Technologies for data fairness and informational freedom in the Internet of Things.
